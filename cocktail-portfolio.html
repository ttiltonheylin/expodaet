About,

how this came to pass..

I was sick and tired of pouring through books and references, (for the same subject over and over again), only to do the same exact thing for the next location I was worked at under a new set of circumstances. I also wanted something I could call my own, while being able to stylize it the way I saw fit. As I began to learn front-end web development, it became more and more clear that building a website was going to be the ultimate expression for my a bar manual.
This collection of knowledge you see before you has been primarily collected by others, from a long list of sources. I will try my darnedest to keep track of all the sources I reference throughout my research here. I hope this manual can be a tool for you and your cohorts, throughout your's and their experience, both as a guest, and staff, illuminating the way through the beverage experience. It is to be noted that this is a work in progress, don't get judgy. lol. If you have feed back just let me know. I you see something that is blatantly wrong and a misstatement of a fact. Express yourself. This manual is meant to be right but sometimes it won't be.
